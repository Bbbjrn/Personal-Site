About Björn Johansson

Björn Johansson is a Salt Lake City-based visual artist specializing in alternative photo printing processes.

Born in 2000, his work explores themes of decay, memory, and the passage of time, using chemical alterations and elemental exposures to reveal the transformative beauty in material breakdown.

With a BFA from Westminster University and experiences at Element Shopworks and Bard College of Berlin, Björn's projects delve into the fragility of experiences and the ephemeral nature of memory, inviting reflection on preservation, loss, and the ways we hold onto the past.
